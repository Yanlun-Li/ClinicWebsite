Yanlun Li Medical Clinic is located in Flushing, Queens, New York. Dr. Li practices the full spectrum of family medicine, seeing patients of all ages, from child to adult, and she also provides care for women’s health. With love and professional skills, she has become the most highly Google rated family doctor in Flushing.

Family Medical Services

  • Internal Medicine: Full physical examination, various common adult medical issues and diseases, including hypertension, heart disease, diabetes, thyroid disease, allergies, liver and stomach diseases, etc.
  • Pediatrics: Entrance physical examination, vaccination, and common diseases in children and adolescents
  • Gynecology: Pap smear, vaginitis, menstrual disorders, emergency contraception, etc.
  • Surgery: Wound cleansing and suture, stitch removal, etc.
  • Medical Examinations: Department of Transportation (DoT) medical examination and Immigration medical examination.
  • COVID-19 vaccine and booster shots: Freely available for both adults and children

Dr. Li is a board-certified family physician. She graduated from the New York Institute of Technology College of Osteopathic Medicine, and she completed her family medicine residency at Wyckoff Heights Medical Center. She is a member of the American Academic of Family Physicians. She is affiliated with North Shore University Hospital and NewYork-Presbyterian Queens Hospital.

Our Mission

The mission of Yanlun Li Medical Practice P.C. is to wholeheartedly serve and improve you and your family’s health. Dr. Li provides medical service based on the principles of honesty and care. Whether it is a routine physical examination or an emergency medical treatment, Dr. Li puts your needs first. She carefully examines the patient, makes accurate diagnoses, and provides effective treatments. Dr. Li does her best to relieve you of the symptoms of the disease.
